# GV.SC-04: Supplier Criticality Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** GV.SC-04 - Suppliers are known and prioritized by criticality

**Scope:** Alma Security 2025 CSF Assessment

**Auditor:** Steve <steve@almasecurity.com>

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review supplier inventory in ServiceNow**
   - Export complete supplier list from ServiceNow
   - Verify all active suppliers are documented
   - Check for orphaned or inactive supplier records

2. **Verify criticality ratings against documented criteria**
   - Review Third Party Risk Management Policy for tiering criteria
   - Sample 5-10 suppliers across tiers and validate classification
   - Confirm criteria include: data sensitivity, system access level, mission importance

3. **Interview procurement team on tiering process**
   - Understand workflow for new supplier classification
   - Document escalation process for high-risk suppliers
   - Verify training on SCRM responsibilities

4. **Sample quarterly review documentation**
   - Pull last two quarterly supplier review reports
   - Verify stakeholder sign-off on criticality ratings
   - Check for rating changes and justification documentation

## Notes

This test procedure validates that the organization maintains an accurate inventory of suppliers and prioritizes them based on risk. The "No PO No Pay" policy ensures all paid suppliers are captured in the inventory.
