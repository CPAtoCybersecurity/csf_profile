- **Ex2:** Examine whether cybersecurity risk strategies that impede operations or innovation should be adjusted

  Alma Security tracks five KPIs quarterly: Time to Detect (TTD), Time to Investigate (TTI), Time to Remediate Crown Jewels (TTR-CJC), Time to Remediate All Systems (TTR-C), and Public Trust Score. Board and management review KPI trends to adjust strategy. Investments in detection capabilities are approved based on demonstrated KPI improvement, balancing security with operational and innovation needs.
