# GV.SC-03: C-SCRM and ERM Integration — Q1 2026 Observation

**Assessment:** 2026 Alma Security CSF Assessment
**Assessor:** Steve Mercer, Internal Audit <steve.mercer@almasecurity.com>
**Observation Date:** 2026-03-13
**Testing Status:** Complete

## Testing Methods

| Method | Performed | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Examine | Yes | Reviewed ERM-C-SCRM alignment documentation, integrated control sets, escalation records |
| Interview | Yes | CISO, CFO |
| Test | No | N/A |

## Findings

### Strengths

- C-SCRM alignment areas documented against enterprise risk management framework
- Supply chain risks in unified risk register alongside internal cybersecurity risks
- Material supply chain risks escalated to senior management through defined criteria
- Improvement process incorporates supply chain lessons learned

### Gaps

- Integrated control set documentation incomplete for recently added supply chain controls
- C-SCRM improvement process findings not systematically fed back into enterprise improvement cycle

## Score

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Actual Score | 5 |
| Target Score | 7 |

**Scoring rationale:** ERM integration scores 5, the floor of the Minimally Acceptable band: the core mechanism executes consistently — supply chain risks sit in the unified risk register alongside internal cybersecurity risks, and material risks escalate to senior management through defined criteria — but with the minor flaws the 5 anchor tolerates. Those flaws are specific: the integrated control set documentation is incomplete for recently added supply chain controls, and C-SCRM improvement findings are not systematically fed back into the enterprise improvement cycle. Reaching Optimized (6) would require the integration itself to be measured — defined indicators the CISO or CFO review and act on — which does not exist today; the two-point gap to the target of 7 makes this the widest shortfall in the GV.SC family alongside incident planning.

## Evidence Reviewed

- ERM-C-SCRM alignment mapping document
- Integrated risk register (supply chain entries)
- Escalation records for material supply chain risks
- C-SCRM improvement action tracker

## Recommendations

| # | Recommendation | Priority | Owner |
|---|----------------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Complete integrated control set documentation | High | GRC Manager |
| 2 | Formalize C-SCRM improvement feedback loop to ERM process | Medium | CISO |
