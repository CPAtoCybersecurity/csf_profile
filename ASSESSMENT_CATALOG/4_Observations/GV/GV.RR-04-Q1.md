# GV.RR-04: Cybersecurity in Human Resources — Q1 2026 Observation

**Assessment:** 2026 Alma Security CSF Assessment
**Assessor:** Steve Mercer, Internal Audit <steve.mercer@almasecurity.com>
**Observation Date:** 2026-03-12
**Testing Status:** Complete

## Testing Methods

| Method | Performed | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Examine | Yes | Reviewed HR processes, background check records, training program, offboarding checklist |
| Interview | Yes | HR Director, CISO |
| Test | Yes | Tested offboarding access revocation for sample of recent departures |

## Findings

### Strengths

- Cybersecurity integrated into all HR lifecycle stages (screening, onboarding, changes, offboarding)
- Background checks conducted for all sensitive roles with biennial refresh
- Security certification funding and career development paths established
- Policy acknowledgment obligations enforced with progressive non-compliance response

### Gaps

- Background check refresh cycle overdue for 5 employees in sensitive roles
- Offboarding access revocation took 48+ hours for 2 of 10 sampled departures (target: 24 hours)

## Score

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Actual Score | 6 |
| Target Score | 7 |

**Scoring rationale:** A 6 (Optimized band) reflects a control that operates consistently across the full HR lifecycle — screening, onboarding, role changes, offboarding — with defined, measured targets such as the 24-hour access revocation SLA and the biennial background check refresh cycle. The measurement is real but the results show tracked exceptions: re-performance testing found 2 of 10 sampled departures exceeding the 24-hour SLA at 48+ hours, and 5 employees in sensitive roles are overdue for refresh checks. Reaching Fully Optimized (7) would require trend data showing these metrics improving over multiple periods; instead Q1 shows SLA misses and an overdue backlog, so the control is measured but not yet demonstrably improving.

## Evidence Reviewed

- HR process documentation (onboarding, offboarding checklists)
- Background check completion records
- Security certification reimbursement records
- Access revocation audit logs (sample of 10 departures)

## Recommendations

| # | Recommendation | Priority | Owner |
|---|----------------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Clear overdue background check refreshes within 30 days | High | HR Director |
| 2 | Automate offboarding access revocation to meet 24-hour SLA | High | Identity Management Lead |
