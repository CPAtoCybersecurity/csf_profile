# GV.RR-01: Leadership Accountability — Q1 2026 Observation

**Assessment:** 2026 Alma Security CSF Assessment
**Assessor:** Steve Mercer, Internal Audit <steve.mercer@almasecurity.com>
**Observation Date:** 2026-03-11
**Testing Status:** Complete

## Testing Methods

| Method | Performed | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Examine | Yes | Reviewed Board charter, CISO authority documentation, culture communications |
| Interview | Yes | CEO, CISO, Board member (Audit Committee chair) |
| Test | No | N/A |

## Findings

### Strengths

- Board, CEO, and CISO roles formally agreed and documented in governance framework
- CEO communicates security culture expectations in all-hands meetings
- CISO directed to maintain comprehensive risk strategy with annual and event-driven reviews
- Semi-annual authority and coordination reviews conducted

### Gaps

- Board member cybersecurity expertise limited; no dedicated cyber advisory board member
- Security culture messaging could be more frequent (currently quarterly)

## Score

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Actual Score | 6 |
| Target Score | 7 |

**Scoring rationale:** A 6 places this control in the Optimized band: leadership accountability executes consistently — Board, CEO, and CISO roles are formally documented in the governance framework — and the semi-annual authority and coordination reviews give the control a defined review mechanism whose owner acts on drift. What keeps the score below the target of 7 (Fully Optimized) is the absence of trend evidence that accountability effectiveness is improving across periods: the Board lacks a member with cybersecurity expertise, and culture messaging remains a quarterly CEO all-hands item rather than a measured, improving program. Until those improvement actions close and multi-period trend data exists, the score cannot cross into Fully Optimized.

## Evidence Reviewed

- Board charter and committee assignments
- CISO job description and authority documentation
- CEO all-hands meeting recordings (Q4 2025, Q1 2026)
- Authority review documentation

## Recommendations

| # | Recommendation | Priority | Owner |
|---|----------------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Recruit Board member with cybersecurity expertise | Medium | CEO |
| 2 | Increase security culture communications to monthly cadence | Low | CISO |
