# GV.PO-01: Cybersecurity Risk Management Policy — Q1 2026 Observation

**Assessment:** 2026 Alma Security CSF Assessment
**Assessor:** Steve Mercer, Internal Audit <steve.mercer@almasecurity.com>
**Observation Date:** 2026-03-07
**Testing Status:** Complete

## Testing Methods

| Method | Performed | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Examine | Yes | Reviewed Information Security Policy, policy approval records, acknowledgment tracking |
| Interview | Yes | CISO, HR Director, sample of department heads |
| Test | Yes | Verified policy acknowledgment completion rates in HRIS |

## Findings

### Strengths

- Comprehensive Information Security Policy approved by CEO and CISO, reviewed annually
- Policy acknowledgment tracked through HRIS with 97% completion rate
- Senior management approval workflow enforced through ServiceNow with digital signatures

### Gaps

- 3% of employees have not acknowledged current policy version (mostly recent hires in onboarding pipeline)
- Supporting procedures referenced in policy not all updated to match latest policy revision

## Score

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Actual Score | 7 |
| Target Score | 8 |

**Scoring rationale:** At 7 this control sits in the Fully Optimized band: the Information Security Policy v4.2 was approved by the CEO and CISO in January 2026 through a ServiceNow workflow with digital signatures, acknowledgment is measured through the HRIS at a 97% completion rate, and Internal Audit independently verified those rates — a control that is executed, measured, and demonstrably managed. It does not reach the 8 anchor (effectiveness high with proportionate cost, sustained over 2+ periods) because two known defects remain open: 3% of employees — chiefly recent hires in the onboarding pipeline — have not acknowledged the current version, and several supporting procedures still lag the v4.2 revision.

## Evidence Reviewed

- Information Security Policy v4.2 (approved January 2026)
- Policy approval workflow records in ServiceNow
- HRIS policy acknowledgment report
- Supporting procedure revision dates

## Recommendations

| # | Recommendation | Priority | Owner |
|---|----------------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Close onboarding acknowledgment gap within 14 days of hire | Low | HR Director |
| 2 | Update supporting procedures to align with policy v4.2 | Medium | Security GRC Lead |
