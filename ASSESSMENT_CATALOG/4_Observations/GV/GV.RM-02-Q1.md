# GV.RM-02: Risk Appetite and Tolerance — Q1 2026 Observation

**Assessment:** 2026 Alma Security CSF Assessment
**Assessor:** Steve Mercer, Internal Audit <steve.mercer@almasecurity.com>
**Observation Date:** 2026-03-08
**Testing Status:** Complete

## Testing Methods

| Method | Performed | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Examine | Yes | Reviewed risk appetite statement, tolerance thresholds, Board ratification records |
| Interview | Yes | CISO, CEO, CFO |
| Test | No | N/A |

## Findings

### Strengths

- Risk appetite statement established by CISO with CEO and CFO input
- Tolerance thresholds quantified for key risk categories (TTD, patching windows, vendor compliance)
- Risk appetite periodically refined based on incident experience and exposure data

### Gaps

- Board ratification of risk appetite statement pending (scheduled Q2 2026)
- No documented example of tolerance thresholds actively driving a risk acceptance or escalation decision

## Score

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Actual Score | 5 |
| Target Score | 7 |

**Scoring rationale:** The 5 rates Minimally Acceptable — the appetite-setting process operates consistently with its exceptions known and tracked, which is the 5 anchor. A risk appetite statement (draft v2.1) exists with CEO and CFO input, and tolerance thresholds are quantified for key categories including TTD, patching windows, and vendor compliance. Optimized (6.0) requires the control to be measured and acted upon, and that evidence is absent on two fronts: Board ratification is still pending (scheduled Q2 2026), and the assessor found no documented instance of a tolerance threshold actually driving a risk acceptance or escalation decision — the thresholds exist on paper but have not demonstrably steered behavior.

## Evidence Reviewed

- Risk appetite statement (draft v2.1)
- Tolerance threshold definitions
- CISO-CFO risk appetite review notes
- Board meeting agenda (Q2 2026 planned items)

## Recommendations

| # | Recommendation | Priority | Owner |
|---|----------------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Complete Board ratification of risk appetite in Q2 2026 | High | CISO |
| 2 | Document at least two cases where tolerance thresholds drove decisions | Medium | GRC Manager |
