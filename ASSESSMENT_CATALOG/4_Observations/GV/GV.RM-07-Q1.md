# GV.RM-07: Strategic Opportunities (Positive Risks) — Q1 2026 Observation

**Assessment:** 2026 Alma Security CSF Assessment
**Assessor:** Steve Mercer, Internal Audit <steve.mercer@almasecurity.com>
**Observation Date:** 2026-03-10
**Testing Status:** Complete

## Testing Methods

| Method | Performed | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Examine | Yes | Reviewed risk register for positive risks, strategy documents, stretch goals |
| Interview | Yes | CISO, CEO |
| Test | No | N/A |

## Findings

### Strengths

- Strategic opportunities identified (early SOC 2 as sales enabler, zero-trust as differentiator)
- Stretch goals documented in cybersecurity strategy with status tracking
- CISO includes opportunity section in quarterly Board presentations

### Gaps

- Positive risks not formally scored alongside negative risks in the risk register
- No systematic method for identifying new strategic opportunities beyond CISO-driven process

## Score

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Actual Score | 5 |
| Target Score | 7 |

**Scoring rationale:** The 5 is Minimally Acceptable: positive-risk identification happens consistently — early SOC 2 as a sales enabler and zero-trust as a market differentiator are documented opportunities, stretch goals are tracked in the cybersecurity strategy, and the CISO presents an opportunity section to the Board each quarter — with the shortfalls known and tracked, which is what the 5 anchor requires. The control misses Optimized (6.0) because it lacks measurement and repeatable process: positive risks carry no formal scoring alongside negative risks in the register, and identification depends entirely on the CISO-driven process rather than a systematic method, so outcomes still hinge on one person's initiative.

## Evidence Reviewed

- Risk register (opportunity entries)
- Cybersecurity strategy stretch goals section
- Board presentation opportunity slides
- Strategy review meeting notes

## Recommendations

| # | Recommendation | Priority | Owner |
|---|----------------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Add positive risk scoring to ServiceNow risk register template | Medium | GRC Manager |
| 2 | Include opportunity identification in quarterly department head surveys | Low | CISO |
