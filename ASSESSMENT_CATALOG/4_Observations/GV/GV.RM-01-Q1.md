# GV.RM-01: Risk Management Objectives — Q1 2026 Observation

**Assessment:** 2026 Alma Security CSF Assessment
**Assessor:** Steve Mercer, Internal Audit <steve.mercer@almasecurity.com>
**Observation Date:** 2026-03-08
**Testing Status:** Complete

## Testing Methods

| Method | Performed | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Examine | Yes | Reviewed cybersecurity strategy, risk management objectives, Board meeting minutes |
| Interview | Yes | CISO, CEO, CFO |
| Test | No | N/A |

## Findings

### Strengths

- Risk management objectives established annually during Q4 strategic planning
- Measurable KPI targets defined (TTD, TTI, TTR) and agreed upon by CISO and CEO
- Senior leaders formally agree on objectives documented in Board meeting minutes

### Gaps

- Objectives not updated after the ongoing Series C funding discussions (Series C is not yet raised) shifted business priorities
- Department-level awareness of risk management objectives limited below VP level

## Score

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Actual Score | 6 |
| Target Score | 7 |

**Scoring rationale:** A 6 places this in the Optimized band: risk management objectives are set annually during Q4 strategic planning, carry measurable KPI targets (TTD, TTI, TTR) agreed between the CISO and CEO, and senior-leader agreement is formally documented in Board meeting minutes — consistent execution with defined measures, per the 6 anchor. The control stops short of Fully Optimized (7.0) because the objectives have not adapted as circumstances changed: they were not revisited when the ongoing Series C funding discussions shifted business priorities, and awareness of the objectives is limited below VP level, leaving no evidence of a maturing, multi-period improvement cycle.

## Evidence Reviewed

- Cybersecurity strategy FY2026
- Risk management objectives document
- Board meeting minutes (Q4 2025)
- KPI target documentation

## Recommendations

| # | Recommendation | Priority | Owner |
|---|----------------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Establish trigger for objective review after major business events | Medium | CISO |
| 2 | Cascade risk objectives to department heads through quarterly briefings | Medium | CISO |
