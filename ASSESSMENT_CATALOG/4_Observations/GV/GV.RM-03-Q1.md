# GV.RM-03: Enterprise Risk Management Integration — Q1 2026 Observation

**Assessment:** 2026 Alma Security CSF Assessment
**Assessor:** Steve Mercer, Internal Audit <steve.mercer@almasecurity.com>
**Observation Date:** 2026-03-09
**Testing Status:** Complete

## Testing Methods

| Method | Performed | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Examine | Yes | Reviewed enterprise risk register, ERM committee minutes, escalation criteria |
| Interview | Yes | CISO, CFO, VP Operations |
| Test | No | N/A |

## Findings

### Strengths

- Cybersecurity risks aggregated alongside financial, operational, and compliance risks in unified register
- CISO participates in enterprise risk management planning sessions
- Escalation criteria defined for elevating cybersecurity risks to enterprise level

### Gaps

- Enterprise risk register format differs slightly between cybersecurity and financial risk entries
- Bottom-up risk identification from non-security teams not formalized

## Score

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Actual Score | 6 |
| Target Score | 7 |

**Scoring rationale:** The 6 corresponds to the Optimized band: cybersecurity risks are aggregated with financial, operational, and compliance risks in a unified ServiceNow register, the CISO sits in ERM planning sessions, and defined escalation criteria govern when cyber risks rise to enterprise level — consistent integration with a reviewed mechanism, satisfying the 6 anchor. Fully Optimized (7.0) would require the integration itself to be demonstrably improving across periods; instead, the register format still differs between cybersecurity and financial risk entries and bottom-up identification from non-security teams remains unformalized, capping the score at 6.

## Evidence Reviewed

- Enterprise risk register in ServiceNow
- ERM committee meeting minutes (Q4 2025, Q1 2026)
- Risk escalation criteria documentation
- CISO quarterly enterprise risk report

## Recommendations

| # | Recommendation | Priority | Owner |
|---|----------------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Standardize risk register format across all risk domains | Medium | CFO |
| 2 | Implement quarterly risk identification surveys for department heads | Medium | CISO |
